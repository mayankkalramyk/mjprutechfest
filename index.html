Vice Chancellor's Message

Inspiring the Future

It gives me immense pleasure to extend my best wishes for the vibrant fest AURA- 2K26 that celebrates creativity, culture, and intellect. Such platforms go beyond competition—they inspire confidence, nurture talent, and encourage students to express ideas, emotions, and innovations with courage and responsibility.

I believe that participation in technical, cultural, and literary activities plays a vital role in shaping balanced personalities and future leaders. May this fest ignite curiosity, foster collaboration, and create memories that motivate them to strive for excellence in every walk of life.

I congratulate the organizing team and wish all participants a joyful, enriching, and successful fest.

Prof. K.P. SinghVice Chancellor, MJPRU

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for the fest?
Registration can be done online through the respective event pages. Click on the event category above to find registration links.
Is accommodation available for outsiders?
Yes, accommodation is provided within the university campus for registered participants on a paid basis. Check the Accommodation section.
Who can participate in the Tech Fest?
Students from any college or university can participate in open events. Some specific events might be restricted to MJPRU students only.
